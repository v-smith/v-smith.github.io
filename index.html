Bio

Hello and welcome to my website! Thank you for visiting! I am a second year PhD student in Biomedical NLP on the CDT for AI Enabled Healthcare Systems at UCL and at the Alan Turing Institute. My current research is focused on extracting Pharmacokinetic Data from tables and free text in Scientific Literature and using this extracted data to predict Pharmacokinetic Parameters. This project is developing skill set in Text Mining, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning and Statistical Data Science.

I am passionate about health and how machine learning and data science can be exploited to improve health outcomes. My career aspiration is to make an impact in health by building computational models which allow better decisions to be made, across drug development, clinical trials and in the clinic, to benefit both patients and health professionals.
