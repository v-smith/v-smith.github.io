Bio

Hello and welcome to my website! Thank you for visiting! I am a third year PhD student in Biomedical Natural Language Processing (NLP) on the CDT for AI Enabled Healthcare Systems at UCL. My current research is focused on extracting Pharmacokinetic Data from tables and free text in Scientific Literature and using this extracted data to predict Pharmacokinetic Parameters. This project is developing my skill set in NLP, Machine Learning and Pharmacometrics

I am passionate about health and how machine learning and data science can be exploited to improve health outcomes. I am also passionate about deploying machine learning safely and ethically. My career aspiration is to make an impact in health by building robust computational models which allow better decisions to be made, across drug development, clinical trials and in the clinic, to benefit both patients and health professionals.
